Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about our services, features, pricing, and support in our comprehensive FAQ section. We've compiled the most common inquiries to help you get started quickly and easily.

  • How do I transfer a domain from one host to another?

    To transfer a domain, unlock it, obtain an authorization code, initiate the transfer with the new host, and confirm the process.

  • Why do I have to wait 60 days to transfer my domain?

    You must wait 60 days due to ICANN regulations to prevent unauthorized domain transfers and ensure security measures are maintained.

  • How do I transfer a domain name to another person?

    To transfer a domain to another person, initiate the transfer through your domain registrar and provide the recipient's information.

  • How do I check the transfer status of my domain?

    To check the transfer status of your domain, log in to your domain registrar's account and view the transfer section.

  • How long does a domain transfer take?

    A domain transfer typically takes 5 to 7 days to complete, depending on the registrar and any verification processes involved.

  • What are some domain transfer limitations?

    Domain transfer limitations may include transfer locks, waiting periods after registration or previous transfers, and specific registrar policies.
